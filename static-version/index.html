The Problem

Healthcare Challenges in Egypt

Millions of Egyptians face fragmented healthcare due to disconnected systems and outdated record-keeping methods.

Lost Medical Records

Paper-based records are easily lost, damaged, or incomplete. Patients carry files between facilities with no centralized system.

Slow Emergency Response

In emergencies, paramedics have no access to patient history, allergies, or current medications, risking lives.

Repeated Tests & Costs

Without shared records, patients undergo redundant lab tests and diagnostics, wasting time and money.

No Data-Driven Insights

Healthcare authorities lack real-time analytics to track disease patterns, allocate resources, or plan interventions.

The Solution

MedLink Connects Everything

MedLink provides a centralized, encrypted platform that gives every Egyptian citizen a smart digital medical record accessible across all healthcare facilities nationwide.

  • One unified digital record per citizen linked to National ID
  • Instant emergency access via QR code - no login required
  • End-to-end encrypted data with role-based access control
  • Real-time healthcare analytics for national planning
  • Seamless collaboration between doctors and facilities
  • Eliminate redundant tests with shared lab results

Features

Everything You Need for Smart Healthcare

Unified Patient Records

Complete medical history in one place. Every diagnosis, prescription, lab result, and visit accessible across all facilities.

QR Emergency Access

Paramedics scan a QR code to instantly view critical info: blood type, allergies, chronic diseases, and current medications.

Secure Encrypted Database

Military-grade AES-256 encryption protects all patient data. HIPAA-compliant security protocols and regular audits.

Role-Based Access Control

Granular permissions for patients, doctors, facilities, and paramedics. Everyone sees only what they need.

National Healthcare Analytics

Real-time dashboards tracking disease patterns, resource allocation, and healthcare metrics across all of Egypt.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Step 01

Register with National ID

Citizens register using their National ID. A unique digital medical record and QR code are generated instantly.

Step 02

Doctor Updates Record

Authorized doctors add diagnoses, prescriptions, lab results, and notes. Everything is securely synced in real-time.

Step 03

Emergency QR Access

In emergencies, paramedics scan the patient's QR code to instantly access critical medical information without login.
